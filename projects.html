PharmAssist

At TreeHacks, we created an app that can take a picture of a medication and the cheapest alternative and its nearest location would pop up. This idea was inspired by those in the lower-middle class to poverty who do not feel like in they can afford what big pharmaceutical companies are pushing as treatment. This experience was really fun because I learned a lot about the medical field, and I made so many new friends from different schools and places. Plus, seeing the Stanford campus was so cool!!

SkyLive

For DubHacks 2022, I implemented the front-end for an iOS app created for skydivers to track various statistics such as air pressure, altitude, and orientation of their dive using Swift while connected to DevEdge hardware. I worked on this project with some of my best friends and we received an Honorable Mention for the T-Mobile Track!

Jream Team

At JPMorgan's Code for Good Hackathon, I collaborated on the front end and backend for a matching algorithm and leaderboard that would imporve tutor retention. This was super fun because I made new friends from different states and I got to see New York City and Times Square afterwards!

NFT Memory Game

I created a web page that allows users to gain NFTs and play a memory game with their new collection. This was one of my first personal projects!

WOCChat

At Athena Hacks, I helped design a mobile app prototype would make customer consultation and analytics easily accessible to women of color owned businesses. This was a really cool experience because half of my team were in different countries!
